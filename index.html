About me

Who am I?

That's a big question! I can start by telling you my name is Florencia Espino, but most people call me Fespi. I'm a UI/UX designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I speak English and Spanish fluently and also some Portuguese.

I consider myself a big design nerd; I love design! I'm always getting inspired by art, movies, videogames, boardgames and just life. I have three cats, I love to travel and I spend most of my free time playing boardgames.

Experience

I have more than 13 years of experience working on all kind of projects for clients all over the world. I love a challenge and learning new skills.

How do I work?

Whenever I'm starting a new or existing project the first question I ask is "who are our users?". My work methodology is to always keep our users in mind and think about how they would interact with our product.

Another important thing about the way I work is that I consider design as a team sport. I've worked in projects both by myself and with a team and I think the best results are always achieved as a team.

My background

I also have a lot of experience working as a Frontend developer, so I use this knowledge not only to communicate with developers, but also to make life easier for them when I'm designing.
