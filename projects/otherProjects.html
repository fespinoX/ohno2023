News Platforms

In 2018, I was part of the design team responsible for crafting the news site covering the G20 summit in Argentina. This platform provided comprehensive coverage of the summit, including updates, event planning details, and information about participating countries.

In 2020, I led the design efforts for the COVID news platform at the Inter-American Development Bank, while also overseeing the redesign of their homepage.

Moreover, I contributed to the design of multiple Web 3 news sites, establishing tailored design systems for each project.
