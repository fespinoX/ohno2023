The project

Since the advent of the AI revolution, I have immersed myself in various AI projects as a designer.

Extensive training has been instrumental in my endeavor to comprehend user interactions with AI, thereby enhancing their experiences.

Leveraging this newfound understanding alongside my expertise in designing self-service platforms, I aim to craft seamless and comprehensive experiences.

The team

These projects engaged both a project manager and developers.

The challenge

Introducing users to new technologies is inherently challenging, as it often requires time for users to build trust in AI and grasp its usage.

This challenge, compounded with the typical obstacles associated with a self-service experience, underscores the necessity for a thorough understanding of UX.

Experience

Airkit

Amidst the AI revolution, Airkit shifted its focus and conceived a generative AI customer service platform. This innovative solution offers fully customizable, autonomous AI agents capable of instantly addressing over 90% of customer inquiries.

I played a pivotal role in designing both the internal platform, tailored for developers, and the Supervisor platform, utilized by customer support supervisors to train the AI.

Salesforce

Salesforce acquired Airkit's AI platform to integrate it into their solution. I am currently a member of the team tasked with seamlessly integrating this service into the Salesforce ecosystem.

Write My Sh*t

Write My Sh*t is a user-friendly self-service platform empowering users to leverage AI for crafting cover letters, resumes, and other job search-related documents effortlessly.

I conceived and designed this platform as a freelance designer.
