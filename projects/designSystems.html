The project

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working on the development and upkeep of comprehensive design systems.

While crafting a design system may initially appear time-intensive, its long-term benefits in efficiency are undeniable.

Design systems play a pivotal role in maintaining visual consistency across various pages and platforms.

The team

The creation and maintanance of design systems is a joint effort between designers and developers, as each component needs to be designed and coded to be reused.

The challenge

Developing a design system presents various challenges. When starting from scratch, anticipation of the necessary components is crucial. Conversely, maintaining an established design system requires meticulous attention to each component to prevent redundancies and uphold consistent principles.

Another significant challenge lies in documentation. It entails thoroughly documenting each component for future reference. While you may be involved in the team responsible for creating a design system, its utilization extends across multiple teams. Thus, comprehensive documentation becomes essential for ensuring that all teams understand how to effectively utilize each component.
